Best Ways to Experience Mount Fuji

  • Climbing Mount Fuji: The official climbing season is from July to early September. This is the best time to trek to the summit and witness the breathtaking sunrise, known as "Goraiko."
  • Lake Kawaguchi: One of the famous Fuji Five Lakes, offering the most picturesque view of Mount Fuji reflected on its serene waters.
  • Chureito Pagoda: A popular photography spot where you can capture Mount Fuji alongside a traditional Japanese pagoda.
  • Fuji-Q Highland: For thrill-seekers, this amusement park near Mount Fuji features some of the world's best roller coasters.
  • Hakone: A perfect getaway to enjoy hot springs with stunning views of Mount Fuji in the backdrop.
